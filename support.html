Getting Started

  1. Select Photos: Tap "Select Photos" to choose photos from your library
  2. Arrange Order: Tap and hold photos to rearrange them in your desired order
  3. Customize Settings: Tap "Continue" to adjust slideshow settings
  4. Create Slideshow: Tap "Create Slideshow" to generate your video
  5. Display on TV: Open Photos app and use AirPlay to cast to your TV

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I display my slideshow on my TV?

After creating your slideshow:

  1. Open the Photos app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Find your slideshow video (it's in your Recents)
  3. Tap the AirPlay icon
  4. Select your Apple TV or AirPlay-compatible TV

Can I reorder my photos?

Yes! On the photo selection screen, tap and hold any photo thumbnail, then drag it to reorder.

How long does it take to create a slideshow?

Processing time depends on the number of photos and settings:

  • 10-30 photos: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
  • 1-hour loop: 2-3 minutes
  • 2-hour loop: 3-5 minutes

Can I create slideshows in the background?

Yes! The app supports background processing. You can minimize the app while your slideshow is being created, and you'll receive a notification when it's ready.

Where are my slideshows saved?

All slideshows are automatically saved to your Photos library in the Recents album.

What's the difference between Fit and Fill?

Slideshow Cast uses "Fit" mode, which displays your entire photo with black bars if needed. This preserves the original aspect ratio of your photos without cropping.

Troubleshooting

Video creation failed

Try these steps:

  • Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Restart the app and try again
  • For very long loops (2 hours), keep the app open during creation

Photos not appearing

Make sure you've granted Photos access:

  1. Open Settings app
  2. Scroll to Slideshow Cast
  3. Tap Photos
  4. Select "All Photos"

AirPlay not working

AirPlay issues are typically related to your network or devices:

  • Ensure your iPhone/iPad and Apple TV are on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Restart your Apple TV
  • Check that AirPlay is enabled on your Apple TV
  • Try playing the video directly in Photos app first
